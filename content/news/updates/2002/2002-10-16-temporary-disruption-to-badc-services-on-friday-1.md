Temporary Disruption to BADC services on Friday 18th October from 8am to noon.
==============================================================================


The power supply to the BADC computer room is being upgraded. This will result 
in the disruption of BADC services from 8am to noon on Friday 18th October. 
In this period data will not be avaliable and the main server is likley to go
down without warning. 

We are sorry for the inconvenience this may cause.

BADC Support 16/10/02.
