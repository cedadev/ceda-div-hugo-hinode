ACSOE data monthly release
==========================

Some data archived in the framework of the ACSOE
project have been released to the public domain:

* ozone profile data (OZPROF) collected in December 1997 and January 1998;
* data from the HILLCLOUD campaigns of June-July 1996 and June-July 1997.

Data directories: * [/badc/acsoe/data/ozprof/aberystwyth/97/](/cgi-bin/data_browser/data_browser/badc/acsoe/data/ozprof/aberystwyth/97/)
                  * [/badc/acsoe/data/ozprof/aberystwyth/98/](/cgi-bin/data_browser/data_browser/badc/acsoe/data/ozprof/aberystwyth/98/)
                  * [/badc/acsoe/data/hillcloud-96/](/cgi-bin/data_browser/data_browser/badc/acsoe/data/hillcloud-96/)
                  * [/badc/acsoe/data/hillcloud-97/](/cgi-bin/data_browser/data_browser/badc/acsoe/data/hillcloud-97/)
